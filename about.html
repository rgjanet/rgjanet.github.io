Hi, I’m Janet Rodríguez and I’m a digital storyteller.

With digital storytelling, it was love at first sight.

Hi! My name is Janet, and I create digital content and social media strategies for companies and entrepreneurs. I also talk about Doctor Who in the podcast Dentro del TARDIS (Spotify, Apple Podcasts).

I'm currently a freelance journalist and digital marketing writer + coordinator at Scoreinc.com, where I produce content for credit repair entrepreneurs and a variety of clients, including Khrysos Industries, Smash Customs, and INX Labs.

When I'm not researching a blog post, taking pictures, or editing a video or podcast episode, I write short stories for my thesis (MA in Creative Writing).
